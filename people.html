Meet the ASPIRE team

We are a team of astronomers based primarily in the US, but our team members are from all over the world. ASPIRE includes professors, research scientists, postdocs, and graduate students who are observational and theoretical experts in quasars, their host galaxies and surrounding environments, high-redshift galaxies, and cosmology.
